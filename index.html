Request Account Deletion - ShopEase - Lista de Compras

Developer: Diego Oliveira Da Fonte

To delete your account and all associated data:

  1. Send an email to diegofontes.002@gmail.com with the subject: "Account Deletion".
  2. In the body of the email, include your registered email address used in the app.

Data to be deleted:

  • Authentication data (Firebase Auth).
  • Product data (Firebase Realtime Database).
  • Product images (Firebase Storage).
  • Any other data generated by the user.

The data will be permanently removed within up to 30 days.
